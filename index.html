About Us

Founded in August 2021 and headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, Erkey Motors was established with a single mission—to build electric vehicles engineered specifically for the demanding needs of last-mile logistics.

While most electric two-wheelers are adapted from commuter platforms, we chose a different path. We designed a purpose-built commercial delivery vehicle from the ground up, focusing on durability, rider ergonomics, operational efficiency, and fleet economics.

Today, our platform features a commercial-grade all-metal architecture, high-speed performance, swappable battery compatibility, and ergonomics optimized for long working hours. Every design decision is driven by one objective: maximizing vehicle uptime and enabling businesses to deliver more with lower operating costs. With thousands of kilometers of real-world testing, multiple intellectual properties, and a commitment to continuous innovation, Erkey Motors is building the next generation of reliable electric workhorses for India's rapidly growing logistics ecosystem and global markets.

We believe the future of mobility isn't just electric—it's purpose-built, dependable, and engineered for productivity.
